Diabetic foot clinic

In order to detect and treat diabetic neuropathy before it worsens, Sehat Hospital provides thorough screening for the illness. Advanced diagnostic instruments including the vascular Doppler for blood flow in the lower limbs, monofilament touch, podiascan for foot pressure distribution, and VPT (Vibration Perception Threshold) are all part of our screening procedure.

These tests aid in identifying any anomalies in the distribution of pressure, blood flow, and nerve function in the feet—all of which are frequently linked to diabetic neuropathy.

The monofilament touch test looks for any loss of sensation in the feet, while the VPT gauges the patient's capacity to detect vibrations. Finding any areas of elevated pressure or possible ulceration may be aided by the non-invasive Podiascan test, which assesses the distribution of pressure on the feet.

In order to assess blood flow in the lower limbs and detect any obstructions or circulation problems that may be a factor in peripheral vascular disorders, Vascular Doppler uses sound waves.In order to avoid consequences like foot ulcers, infections, and amputations, diabetic neuropathy and peripheral vascular disorders must be identified and treated early. We can detect any problems early on and provide individualized treatment strategies to properly manage the illness thanks to our thorough screening procedure.

ECG AND TMT

Treadmill Test (TMT) and Electrocardiogram (ECG) are widely utilized screening methods for detecting cardiac disease in individuals with diabetes.

Given that diabetes significantly increases the risk of cardiovascular conditions, those affected by diabetes are at a higher likelihood of developing heart disease compared to non-diabetic individuals.

Consequently, it is crucial to conduct regular screenings for cardiac issues in diabetic patients to avert complications and facilitate timely interventions. The TMT is a non-invasive procedure that assesses the heart's performance under physical stress.

During this assessment, the patient walks on a treadmill while their heart rate, blood pressure, and ECG readings are continuously monitored. This test can reveal any irregularities in the heart's electrical activity, such as arrhythmias or ischemia, which may suggest underlying heart disease.

At Sehat Hospital, TMT and ECG screenings are routinely conducted for diabetic patients to evaluate their cardiac health. The hospital's medical team collaborates to analyze the results and develop suitable treatment and management strategies for patients. By offering consistent cardiac screenings for those with diabetes, the hospital contributes to the prevention of complications and enhances the overall health and quality of life for its patients.

Glucose monitoring and CGMS

Insulin pumps

At Sehat Hospital, our commitment is to deliver the most advanced and effective treatments for individuals managing diabetes. We are excited to introduce the innovative Insulin Pump, a state-of-the-art device aimed at enhancing diabetes management and improving our patients' quality of life. This compact, portable device administers precise doses of insulin continuously throughout the day, thereby eliminating the necessity for multiple daily injections. The Insulin Pump offers increased convenience, flexibility, and accuracy in insulin delivery. Our team of highly trained endocrinologists and diabetes care specialists is well-versed in the latest techniques and protocols for the successful implementation and use of the Insulin Pump.

Insulin Pump boasts several key features, including:

Continuous Insulin Delivery: This device ensures a consistent flow of insulin, closely resembling the body's natural insulin production, which leads to improved glucose control.

Customizable Settings: Our team collaborates with you to tailor the insulin pump settings to meet your specific needs, lifestyle, and treatment objectives.

Integrated Glucose Monitoring: Certain models of the Insulin Pump come equipped with continuous glucose monitoring (CGM) capabilities, providing real-time blood glucose readings and trends to facilitate informed insulin dosing decisions.

Bolus Dose Flexibility: The Insulin Pump allows for accurate mealtime insulin dosing, enabling easy adjustments and additional insulin delivery as required.

By adopting the Insulin Pump, patients can achieve better blood glucose management, experience fewer hypoglycemic episodes, and enjoy enhanced overall diabetes control. Our dedicated diabetes care team will assist you in incorporating the Insulin Pump into your diabetes management plan, offering comprehensive education, support, and continuous monitoring to ensure optimal outcomes. At Sehat Hospital, we are devoted to leveraging the latest advancements in diabetes care to empower our patients to lead fulfilling lives.
